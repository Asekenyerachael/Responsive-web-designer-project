THE FIX ME APP

About

The fix me app is the website based company that helps to ease home living by simplifying the means of accessing service providers in society today. The company is designed to enable clients access the most trust worthy handy men service providers in the market The service providers are normally pre-screened before their profiles are uploaded on to the website for the best interest of both parties. The company is devloped in the interest to create more employment Opportunities for the increasing number of the unemployed youth in society today.

The Mission Statement

To create improve home living in society today while narrowing the gaps in job society in the community today

The Vision Statement

To create aclear and concise enviroment of trust between handy man services and their clients
